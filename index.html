Welcome

Optimalbnb is an online service that empowers Airbnb hosts in Berlin, Germany with the ability to price their listings using the insight of our powerful data science algorithms and vast records of previous Berlin area Airbnb listings. This ensures that our users are able to price their listings at a level that is attractive to potential visitors, while still charging an amount high enough to maximize revenue. If you are a Berlin based Airbnb host or would like to become one, we invite you to sign up for an account with us and get started. We look forward to working with you.

Get Started With Optimalbnb

Create an account

Click the 'Sign Up' button on the upper-right corner of your screen. Enter a username and password combination that you will use to access your account.

Create a listing

Tell us a few things about the residence that you would like to rent on Airbnb, so that we may provide you with an optimal price for you to set.

Be a great host

Our service will enable you to price your listing at a competitive rate, but getting good reviews for your service from visitors will also be crucial to your success as an Airbnb host.
